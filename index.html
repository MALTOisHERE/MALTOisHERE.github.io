About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I'm an Information Systems Security Engineer passionate about breaking, securing, and improving systems so organizations don’t have to worry about others breaking them maliciously.

My background spans network security, ethical hacking, penetration testing, and secure software development. Today, my focus is on understanding and mitigating cyber threats through practical offensive and defensive security work. I specialize in identifying vulnerabilities across networks, applications, and infrastructures, conducting security audits and adversary simulations, and developing secure, efficient systems that strengthen an organization’s overall security posture. I enjoy turning complex security challenges into innovative and practical solutions that protect sensitive data and improve resilience.

Cybersecurity is more than a profession for me—it’s something I genuinely enjoy. I constantly explore new attack techniques, study real-world vulnerabilities, and expand my knowledge of offensive strategies. The process of discovering weaknesses, understanding how attackers think, and helping organizations stay ahead of emerging threats motivates me every day.

I’m always open to meaningful discussions, challenging projects, and new opportunities within cybersecurity, offensive security, and secure system design. If you’re looking for someone who can help test and improve your defenses—or if you simply want to exchange ideas about security, technology, and problem-solving—feel free to reach out.
